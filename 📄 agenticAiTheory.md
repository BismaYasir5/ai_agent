# Agentic AI

## What is Agentic AI?
Agentic AI is a type of AI that can make decisions and use tools to complete tasks instead of only replying with text.

## Difference from a Normal Chatbot
A normal chatbot only gives answers. Agentic AI can take actions like calling APIs and fetching live data.

## Real-Life Example
A smart assistant that checks live traffic and weather before planning a travel route.
